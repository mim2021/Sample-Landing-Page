Overview

Sample Internet Technologies Ltd. established in 2021 a registered and listed entity with the Office of Registrar of Joint Stock Companies & Firms Bangladesh, aspires to become one of the leading Internet Business Based Group.Primarily, Sample Internet Technologies Ltd. operates with the goal to play a significant part in the advancement towards a Digital Bangladesh. With this in mind, Sample Internet Technologies Ltd. is working with multiple ventures and at the same time, seeking out innovative and unique Internet based Business Initiatives that will align with the company’s mission and vision. Using such projects, Sample Internet Technologies Ltd. will serve the emerging Internet Using Customers with Portals that will fulfill their wants and needs, enhancing their convenience and thereby taking one sustainable step at a time towards the future.
